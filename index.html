Puzzle #1 : Kenny's Mishaps

It's a bright and sunny day at the Jurong Lake Gardens and young Kenny has taken notes on several things! Can you help him decipher the notes that he has taken?

*** Clues ***

  1. The tree with thick leaves, the tree with white flowers, and the tree that is used for making furniture are all different trees.
  2. The tree that is used for making fish poison doesn't have white flowers.
  3. Of the tree with thick leaves and the tree with white flowers, one is used to make fish poison.
  4. The tree with thick leaves cannot be used to make fish poison.
